## Celebration at Children's Creativity Center

 JUSTBR

Republic Day was celebrated with great pride and patriotic spirit on 26 January 2026 at the Childrens Creativity Center, an initiative of Global Concerns India. The program commemorated the historic day when the Constitution of India came into force, marking India's transformation into a sovereign republic on 26 January 1950.

The program also featured a fancy dress narration by Abhi, who paid tribute to the great leaders behind the making of the Constitution, including Dr. B. R. Ambedkar, Mahatma Gandhi, Jawaharlal Nehru, Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the freedom fighters, and the members of the Constituent Assembly whose sacrifices shaped the nation. JUSTBR JUSTBR
